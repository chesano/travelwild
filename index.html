Birding tours

India is a vast South Asian country with diverse terrain – from Himalayan peaks to Indian Ocean coastline. India has vast variety and species of birds. You will be able to find more than 1400 species of birds across India, spread over different terrains and habitats. There are over 925 breeding species (~920 residents). The Indian birds belong to 25 groups called 'Orders', which are further divided into 'Families', 'Sub-families' and 'Genera'. For birding in India, 13 bio-geographical regions can be demarcated: Trans Himalayan, Western Himalayas, Eastern Himalayas, Desert, Semi-arid, Gangetic plain, Central India, Deccan Plateau, Western Ghats, Eastern Ghats, North East, Coasts and the Andaman Nicobar Islands. The long coastline and the island territories provide an opportunity to watch and study a large number of pelagic birds.

Wildlife Tours

India is famous for its rich and abundant wilderness, and a trip exploring the vast and diversified wildlife is a must when one is visiting India. One can witness hundreds of diverse genus of creatures viz. tiger, lion, leopard, elephant, bear, wild, jungle cat, jackal, wolf, hyena and many more in their most raw and natural habitat. Not only this, wildlife tours bring you close to different sorts of vegetation and other beautiful creations of nature which make your holidays in India more appealing and adventurous.
