Most of us are too busy to head out for birding or watch wildlife during the week. A weekend getaway with a group of friends/like minded people can be one of the most fun things you can do, but the planning can be a huge headache. With too many opinions and too many differing schedules, it’s extremely difficult to organize.

Customized tours for weekends include

Weekend Birding Bird watching and photography with naturalist and guide .

Weekend Wildlife safaris Tailored made for enjoying local wildlife and understanding more about them.

Nature trails Walks and treks designed to let you connect with nature, take nothing but photographs, leave nothing but footprints.

Monsoon waterfall tours enjoy the scenic beauty of waterfalls at the times when they are at their best..

Jungle Stays Listen to the natures music, inhale the aroma and fragnance of forest while you stay in some of the off beat Jungle stays .

Yoga Some exercise is always necessary and what better exercise than Yoga? Learn and enjoy Yoga, cleanse yourself in one of the best possible ways.
