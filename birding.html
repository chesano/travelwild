Bird watching is great hobby leading you to a more up close and personal interaction with Mother nature. Bird watching helps develop a good vision and sight in you. It also helps you interact with like minded people who have similar passion, love and appreciation for the beauty that nature, wild life and birds offer. It gives strength to your inner soul and offers you peace and solace in the most natural ways keeping you utmost close to Mother nature.

Birding Tours Organized so far

Nal Sarovar (Gujarat) is a habitat to over 210 species of migratory birds.

Little Rann of Kutch(Gujarat) which attracts a variety of dry land birds ranging from larks to eagles.

Greater Rann of Kutch (Gujarat) a famous attraction for grasslands and scrubland birds.

Velavadar Black buck sanctuary (Gujarat) which is a main attraction for enjoying a sight at the Black buck,Pelicans,Desart birds,Eagels,Wolf,hyena.

Chopta (Uttarakhand) located near the mighty Himalayas, this location is known for many migratory birds which are usually found in cold regions.

Sattal (Himachal Pradesh) a region in lower Himalayan range is a habitat for more than 500 species of migratory birds.

Sokhaliya( Ajmer) this region is famous for the endangered species of birds called as the Lesser Florican.

Bharatpur (Rajasthan) also known as the ‘Ornithologist’s Paradise’ Bharatpur is home to over 400 species of birds.

DNP –Desert National Park ( Rajasthan) presently is the most important site for the survival of the globally threatened (critically endangered) Great Indian Bustard (GIB).

Mahavir bird sanctuary ( Goa ) variety of species like black woodpecker, Malabar pied hornbills and more then 400 species of birds can be found.
